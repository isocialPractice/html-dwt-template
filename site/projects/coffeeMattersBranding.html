Coffee Matters Branding

Coffee Matters Branding System

This brand system defines the Coffee Matters identity—logomark, wordmark, typography, and an approachable color palette—supported by scalable usage rules. Assets are optimized for web and print, with guidance for social, packaging, and storefront signage to keep the brand consistent across touchpoints.

Initial Design I

Initial Design I

We developed a flexible brand system for a coffee machine manufacturer, centering the steaming‑cup icon inside a clean circular badge. The mark scales cleanly from app UI to packaging, equipment decals, and trade‑show signage.
Typography, spacing, and color tokens define a warm, trustworthy palette and consistent rhythm across touchpoints. The guidelines include safe‑area rules, misuse examples, and export presets for both print and web.

Removing Elements and Changing Colors

Initial Design II

We audited early directions and trimmed decorative elements so the mark remains strong in small and high‑wear applications. Color studies explored warm neutrals, espresso browns, and an accent blue that signals freshness.
The final badge system balances personality with manufacturing constraints like embossing, silk‑screen, and laser etching. The result is consistent, legible, and easy for teams to reproduce worldwide.

Variation of Elements and Colors

Initial Design III

We iterated on badge forms, ribbon spans, and the logotype’s arc to preserve legibility while keeping a friendly tone. Variants were evaluated on control panels, bean hoppers, and cartons to catch real‑world scaling issues.
Substrate tests informed final stroke weights and safe contrast ranges to meet manufacturing limits. The guidelines now include approved lockups and misuse examples to prevent distortion or color drift.

Final Design Colors and Elements

Final Design

The final system standardizes color tokens, spacing, and component naming for effortless reuse across teams. We documented primary and secondary lockups, icon usage, and approved color pairs with accessibility checks.
A production‑ready export kit ships vectors, print profiles, and optimized bitmaps at key sizes. With these assets, internal and partner teams can deploy the brand consistently across hardware, packaging, and digital surfaces.
